## Course Overview

AI Fundamentals is a comprehensive 4-week intensive course designed to provide a solid understanding of artificial intelligence concepts, techniques, and applications. The curriculum bridges theoretical foundations with practical implementations, making AI accessible to both technical and non-technical audiences.

### Course Goals
- Master AI/ML/DL fundamentals and their relationships
- Understand core problem-solving algorithms and search methods
- Build practical machine learning and deep learning models
- Explore generative AI, ethics, and strategic AI adoption
- Qualify for industry-recognized PCAP certification

### Learning Outcomes
Upon successful completion, students will be able to:
1. Define AI/ML/DL and articulate their relationships
2. Explain AI history from origins to modern breakthroughs
3. Identify core AI problem-solving methods and algorithms
4. Distinguish between supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning
5. Understand neural network architectures (CNNs, RNNs, Transformers)
6. Evaluate AI models using proper metrics and validation
7. Describe real-world AI applications across industries
8. Understand LLMs and Generative AI capabilities
9. Analyze ethical implications of AI systems
10. Develop strategic perspective on AI adoption
