## Certification

**Proxiant Certified AI Professional (PCAP)** validates mastery of AI concepts, tools, and applications.

**PCAP exam:**
- Duration: 3 hours
- Questions: 100 multiple choice
- Pass score: 70%
- Valid for: 3 years
- Cost: $199
- Eligibility: pass any Academy course with a 70% or higher weighted average

**Bootcamp credentials:** each bootcamp has its own certification exam (60 MCQs plus 4 scenario questions, 240 points, 70% to pass) and issues its own credential: **PCAP-Agents** for the agents bootcamp, **PCAP-RAG** for the RAG bootcamp.

**Benefits:**
- Industry-recognized credential with a verifiable digital badge
- Access to the alumni network and Discord community
- Job placement assistance
- Continuing education credits

## FAQ

**Do I need prior AI or programming experience?**
No. The Fundamentals course is designed for complete beginners. Intermediate and advanced courses assume basic familiarity with concepts covered in Fundamentals. The bootcamps expect working Python and prior LLM exposure.

**What's the time commitment for each course?**
Self-paced courses typically require 8–12 hours per week. Live cohort courses have scheduled class times plus assignments. 10–15 hours per week is recommended for optimal learning.

**Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?**
Yes. All courses carry a 14-day money-back guarantee.

**Is the PCAP certification recognized by employers?**
Yes. The PCAP is recognized by leading enterprises and demonstrates professional competence in AI.

**What if I need to pause my course?**
Self-paced courses offer lifetime access. Live cohort courses offer deferrals to future cohorts.

**Do you offer payment plans?**
Yes. Flexible monthly installment plans with no interest.

**Can I audit a course without the certification?**
Yes, at a reduced price. Audits include all materials and lectures but not graded assignments or certification.

**What if I have questions during the course?**
All students have access to the community forum, live Q&A sessions, and instructor office hours.
